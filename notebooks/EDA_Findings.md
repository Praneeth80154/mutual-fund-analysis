### EDA Finding 1

Daily NAV values across most mutual fund schemes showed a long-term upward trend from 2022 to 2026, indicating sustained market growth despite periodic corrections.

### EDA Finding 2

SBI Mutual Fund consistently maintained the highest Assets Under Management (AUM), highlighting its dominant market position.

### EDA Finding 3

Monthly SIP inflows increased steadily throughout the study period and reached an all-time high in December 2025, reflecting growing retail investor confidence.

### EDA Finding 4

Flexi Cap, Mid Cap, and Large & Mid Cap categories attracted the highest net inflows, indicating investor preference for growth-oriented equity funds.

### EDA Finding 5

The majority of investors belong to the 26–45 age group, suggesting that working professionals form the core mutual fund investor base.


### EDA Finding 6

Investment amounts generally increase with age, showing that experienced investors tend to allocate larger capital to mutual funds.

### EDA Finding 7

T30 cities contribute a significant share of total investments, although participation from B30 cities has also increased over time.

### EDA Finding 8

The mutual fund industry folio count nearly doubled between 2022 and 2025, demonstrating rapid expansion in investor participation.

### EDA Finding 9

Equity funds dominate industry folios and continue to grow faster than debt and hybrid fund categories.

### EDA Finding 10

Banking, Financial Services, Technology, and diversified sectors account for a major portion of mutual fund portfolio allocations, indicating strong exposure to India's key growth sectors.
